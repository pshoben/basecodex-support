Privacy Policy

Applies to StackySnails, WormyBop and GrammarGlow. Last updated 15 July 2026.

Short version: our apps don't collect your personal data. Everything an app stores — your progress, scores, settings and learning history — stays on your device and, where the app supports it, in your own private iCloud account. We can't see it, we don't sell it, and we don't share it.

What we collect

Nothing that identifies you. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not gather names, email addresses, contacts, location, advertising identifiers or usage-tracking profiles.

Data stored on your device

To work, an app saves your game or learning data (progress, scores, preferences) locally on your device. If the app offers iCloud sync, that data is stored in your own iCloud account under your Apple ID and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We have no access to it.

Analytics & tracking

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs and we do not track you across other apps or websites. If Apple provides us with anonymous, aggregated App Store or crash statistics, that data is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to identify you.

Purchases

Any purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details.

Children

Our apps are suitable for a general audience and do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Because we collect no personal data at all, there is nothing about a child (or anyone) for us to hold.

Your rights

Since the data lives on your device / in your iCloud, you are in control: deleting the app (and clearing its iCloud data from Settings) removes it. If you have any questions about your privacy, email us any time.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
